Ministry Regulation Q&A System

Lead ML Engineer

🎯 Problem

Citizens struggled to navigate 10,000+ pages of Arabic government regulations, leading to 500+ daily support calls and compliance confusion.

👥 Users & Stakeholders

3,000+ daily users (citizens, lawyers, civil servants). Ministry of Justice stakeholders demanding 95%+ accuracy.
